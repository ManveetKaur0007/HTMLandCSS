The various comapnies to save the environment

Green Envy

Green Envy is Windsor’s ONLY bulk refill shop, located in Olde Riverside! They specialize in Eco-Friendly, Cruelty-Free, Vegan and Zero-Waste products. They feature 28 amazing products at their refill station such as Shampoos, Conditioners, Cleaners and so much more!!


RWDI

They help clients tackle complex challenges in the built environment by combining innovative thinking, collaborative problem solving, and a passion for expanding the boundaries of the possible.


Superior Environmental Services

They help your company reach it’s environmental goals. They are a professional, experienced waste transportation and disposal company, licensed by the Ministry of the Environment to transport hazardous and non-hazardous liquid and solid waste.


Windsor of Change

Windsor of Change is a non-profit that aims to empower the Windsor-Essex community, through education and engagement, to act and adapt to climate change and define its own unique climate action identity.
